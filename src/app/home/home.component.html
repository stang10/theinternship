The Internship was released in 2013. It stars Vince Vaughn and Owen Wilson who play middle aged men feeling outdated in the world around them. After being laid off, they seek out work in other fields. After an unconventional interview and a little help from some sympathetic judges, these men embark on their journey as "Nooglers" otherwise known as Google Interns.
